About

This website is dedicated to providing a comprehensive platform centered around the renowned Impressionist artist, Claude Monet. It aims to offer an enriching and engaging experience for art enthusiasts, students, researchers, and the general public interested in exploring Monet's life, artworks, and related exhibition information.

The Map section serves to visualize the locations of Monet's paintings, facilitating scholars in comparing similarities and contrasts between works from different areas. It enables analysis of the development of Monet's themes and artistic techniques.
